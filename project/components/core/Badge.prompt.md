Compact uppercase status/category pill. Tone is meaning, never decoration.

```jsx
<Badge tone="pos" dot>Recommandé</Badge>
<Badge tone="info">Institutionnel</Badge>
<Badge tone="warn">Earnings proche</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" size="sm">cash-settled</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral · accent · pos · neg · warn · info`. Sizes `sm | md`. `dot` adds a leading status dot.
